Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 1, 2026

Proud Daily ("the App") is developed and operated by Chad Newbry, LLC ("we", "us", "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the App.

Information We Collect

Proud Daily is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, transmit, or store your personal data on external servers. All data stays on your device (and optionally in your personal iCloud account).

The App may access the following on-device data with your permission:

  • HealthKit Data: Mindfulness minutes, used solely to track your progress within the App. This data is never shared with third parties or transmitted off your device beyond your personal iCloud sync.
  • Microphone: Used only for voice journaling. Audio is processed and stored locally on your device.
  • Photo Library: Used to personalize your journal entries. Photos you select remain on your device.
  • Journal Entries & Affirmations: All journal entries, affirmations, favorites, and daily records are stored locally using SwiftData.

iCloud Sync

If you enable iCloud sync, your data is stored in your personal iCloud account managed by Apple. We do not have access to your iCloud data. Apple's privacy policy governs iCloud storage.

Analytics & Tracking

Proud Daily does not include any third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking tools. We do not track you.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transfer your personal data to any third party.

Data Retention & Deletion

All data is stored on your device. Deleting the App removes all locally stored data. If you use iCloud sync, you can manage or delete that data through your iCloud settings.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
