Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sync my data across devices?

Enable iCloud sync in the app's Settings. Your data will sync across all devices signed into the same iCloud account.

How do I delete my data?

Deleting the app removes all locally stored data. If you have iCloud sync enabled, manage your data through Settings → Apple ID → iCloud on your device.

Is my data private?

Yes. All your data stays on your device (and optionally in your personal iCloud account). We do not collect or share any of your personal data. See our Privacy Policy for details.

The app isn't working correctly. What should I do?

  • Make sure you're running the latest version from the App Store.
  • Try restarting the app or your device.
  • If the issue persists, email us with a description and your iOS version.
