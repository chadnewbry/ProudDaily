Proud Daily works without any account or login. Simply open the app and you'll see your daily affirmation immediately.

5 free affirmations are available without any purchase. After that, users can unlock the full library with a one-time $4.99 in-app purchase (non-consumable).

There is no server-side component — all affirmation content is bundled within the app. iCloud sync is optional and uses the user's private CloudKit container.

No special configuration or demo account is needed to review the app.
